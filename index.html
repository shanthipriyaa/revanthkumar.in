About Dr. Revanth Kumar

Dr. Puranam Revanth Kumar is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning at Malla Reddy University, Hyderabad, India. He received his PhD degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from ICFAI University, Hyderabad, India, in 2024. He received his Master of Technology (M.Tech) degree in Control and Instrumentation from AMRITA University, Coimbatore, India, in 2019 and his Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering from GITAM University, Hyderabad, India, in 2016.

He is also an active Member of the IEEE Computer Society. His research interests include Image processing, Neuroimaging, Parallel Computing, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. He is also the reviewer of Artificial Intelligence Review, Intelligence Medicine, Journal of Super Computing, and EAI Transactions on Intelligent Systems and Machine Learning.
